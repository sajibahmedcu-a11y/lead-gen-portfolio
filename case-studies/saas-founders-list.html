The challenge

The client had just closed their Series A and wanted to move upmarket from self-serve signups to founder-led outbound. Their internal list was a messy export of 4,000+ rows pulled from old webinars and trade shows: duplicated, missing titles, and full of generic info@ inboxes. Their previous cold campaign had bounced at over 20%, which was hurting their sender reputation.

They needed a clean, tightly targeted list of founders, CEOs and heads of growth at B2B SaaS companies with 11–200 employees in the US, UK and Canada, with verified work emails and accurate titles, so their two SDRs could focus on selling instead of cleaning data.

My approach

I broke the project into five repeatable stages so the client could see progress every week and approve the targeting before I scaled list building.

  1. 1. Defined the Ideal Customer Profile

    Worked with the client to lock the ICP: B2B SaaS, 11–200 employees, US/UK/CA, with job titles limited to Founder, Co-Founder, CEO, VP Growth and Head of Marketing. We excluded agencies and non-software companies to keep relevance high.

  2. 2. Sourced prospects

    Built saved searches in LinkedIn Sales Navigator and cross-referenced with Apollo to capture companies matching the firmographics. I prioritised companies showing buying signals (recent funding, active hiring for sales roles).

  3. 3. Enriched & structured the data

    For each contact I captured full name, title, company, company size, LinkedIn URL, work email and a one-line personalisation note. Everything went into a single, consistently formatted Google Sheet ready for CRM import.

  4. 4. Verified every email

    Ran the full list through NeverBounce, then manually pattern-checked the few unknowns. Any catch-all or risky address was flagged so the SDRs could decide whether to use it, protecting domain reputation.

  5. 5. Deduplicated & QA'd

    Removed duplicates against the client's existing CRM, dropped role-based inboxes, and did a final 10% spot-check by hand to confirm titles and company matches before delivery.

Deliverables

  • 1,200 verified founder & decision-maker contacts
  • CRM-ready CSV plus a live Google Sheet
  • Personalisation note per contact for warmer outreach
  • Verification report with deliverability status per row

Sajib delivered a clean, verified list ahead of schedule. Our bounce rate dropped from over 20% to under 2%, and the team booked 14 meetings from the first send.

— Marketing Lead, US B2B SaaS

The results

The client launched their first founder-led campaign two weeks after delivery. With a clean list and per-contact personalisation, the numbers spoke for themselves:

  • 98% deliverability — bounce rate fell from 20%+ to under 2%
  • 41% open rate on the opening email sequence
  • 14 meetings booked within the first 6 weeks
