The challenge

The agency had a strong portfolio but relied entirely on referrals for new business. When referrals slowed, their pipeline dried up. They'd tried mass LinkedIn connection blasts before, copy-pasting the same generic pitch, and it produced almost nothing but a sub-3% reply rate and a few annoyed prospects.

They wanted predictable, founder-led outreach that felt human, targeted only marketing decision-makers at e-commerce brands doing $1M–$20M in revenue, and protected the founder's personal brand. The goal was simple: book qualified discovery calls.

My approach

Instead of spraying connection requests, I built a tight, personalised system around one principle: relevance beats volume. Every step was tracked in a Notion board the client could check daily.

  1. 1. Pinpointed the audience

    Built Sales Navigator searches for Founders, CMOs and Heads of Marketing at e-commerce brands in the US/UK, filtered by company size and revenue signals. I capped the target at 500 genuinely high-fit prospects rather than thousands of loose ones.

  2. 2. Researched personalisation hooks

    For each prospect I logged a specific opener: a recent product launch, a hiring post, a podcast appearance or a campaign they'd run. This became the first line of every message so it never read like a template.

  3. 3. Built a 4-touch sequence

    A soft personalised connection note, a value-first follow-up referencing their hook, a short case-style proof point, and a respectful break-up message. No hard pitch until the prospect engaged.

  4. 4. Paced sending to stay safe

    Kept daily connection requests within LinkedIn's safe limits to protect the account and avoid the spam filter, sending in small, consistent batches across the week.

  5. 5. Tracked, tested & iterated

    Logged accepts, replies and positive responses weekly. I A/B tested two opening lines, kept the stronger one, and refined the follow-up timing based on when replies actually landed.

Deliverables

  • 500 researched, high-fit prospects with personalisation notes
  • A 4-touch message sequence written for the founder's voice
  • A live Notion tracker for connections, replies and booked calls
  • Weekly performance reports with iteration notes
